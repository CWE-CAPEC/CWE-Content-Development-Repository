NAME: CWE-50 modification add CanPrecede of CWE-601

DESCRIPTION:

We suggest adding a CanPrecede relation of CWE-601. Similarly, CWE-601
should document CanFollow of CWE-50.

Context: There has been multiple open-redirect CVE entries caused by
mistreatment of double slashes in the Location header. Those programs
generally accept user input that is prefixed with a slash, and use it
directly for redirecting purposes. Because double-slash-prefixed URLs are
schema-relative and not origin-relative, this produces open redirect.

Example CVEs: CVE-2025-54793, CVE-2021-23387, CVE-2021-22964,
