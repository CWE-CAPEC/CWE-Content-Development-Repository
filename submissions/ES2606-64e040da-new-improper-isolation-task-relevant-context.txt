SUBMISSION TYPE: Software

NAME: Improper Isolation of Task-Relevant Context

DESCRIPTION:

The output quality, correctness, or security of an AI model is influenced
by information that is unrelated to the task being performed (i.e. user
prompt). The model fails to reliably separate the requested task from
surrounding contextual cues, causing irrelevant topics, words, or concepts
to alter its behavior. As a result, the same task may produce significantly
different outcomes depending on unrelated prompt content. This can lead to
degraded performance, incorrect results, or security vulnerabilities that
are difficult for users to anticipate or detect.
